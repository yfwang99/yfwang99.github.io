# Innovation

Aerodynamics Reimagined.

![local model fig4](/images/airmodel/figure4.jpg )


| **Closed-loop Stability**<br><small style="color:#999">Onboard IMU and GPS sensing to continuously adjust thrust and control surfaces.</small> | **Ventral Keel Fin**<br><small style="color:#999">Underbody stabilizing surface added to increase yaw damping and directional stability.</small> |
| ---                                                                                                                                    | ---                                                                                                                            |
| **Integrated Payload Bay**<br><small style="color:#999">Internal fuselage cabin that keeps mass near the center of gravity and protects cargo.</small> | **Wing-area Optimization**<br><small style="color:#999">Balanced wing geometry to strengthen ground-effect without adding unnecessary drag.</small> |
